As a general rule, all custom and original plugins are completely free and open for anyone to use. However clones (PFA, miditrail, etc.) will be kept private and only handed out to people I trust, to prevent overuse.

I am actively working on new plugins, and it is possible for anyone else to make a plugin themselves as well.
